Camelback Truss Design
Our Design with spaghetti

In the first round of testing, the curved truss bridges performed the best. For this reason, we decided to try a camelback truss design for our second bridge. Because our last bridge was too heavy, we used fewer spaghetti layers. We used 3 spaghetti strands in areas with compression and 2 spaghetti strands in areas with tension. To better meet the size requirements, we also lowered the height, length and width of our bridge.
