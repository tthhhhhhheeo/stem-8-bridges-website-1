Bridge #1

Design Process

step 1: make the template

step 2: lay out spaghetti on the template

step 3: glue together joints

step 4: repeat step 1, and 2

step 5: glue together (crossmember)

Results

our bridge weiged 107 grams and held 10 pounds of force from the crushing mashine.

this gives us a score of 42.5 points. which landed us first place.

Reflections and Improvements

we should have made the arch of the bridge thicker,

and made more joints so the spaghetti was shorter.
