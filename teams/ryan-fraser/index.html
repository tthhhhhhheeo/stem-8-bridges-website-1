Bridge Design Journal

This is our bridge design journal. For our project, we were given a limit of:

- 250 grams of spaghetti

- 5 hot glue sticks

- maximum width- 10 cm

- maximum height- 20 cm

- maximum length- 60 cm

The objective of this project is to make a bridge that holds as much weight as possible.
